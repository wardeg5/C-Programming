Getting Started Assignment

This is your first graded assignment, and the only assignment that does not involve programming.  The purpose of this assignment is to introduce yourself to the class and instructor, and provide information that can help us ensure your success in this course.  You will upload 2 files to the Desire2Learn dropbox and then introduce yourself on the discussion board.

In this assignment the first thing you should do is create a text file (a Notepad document, not a Word document) that contains your answers to the following questions:

  1. Why are you taking CS 133U?
  2. What type of programming experience do you have?
  3. What type of system (Windows, Linux, Apple, etc.) will you use to develop programs?
  4. What IDE (DevC++, Visual Studio, Xcode, etc.) will you use to develop programs?
  5. List the assignments and their submission dates.
  6. List the required exams and their end dates.
  7. How long are the exams open? For a week? month? term?
  8. How many attempts are you allowed for each mandatory exam? Which score counts towards your grade?
  9. List all the places on Desire2Learn where the instructor might put assignment help for you? (hint: discussion is one of em)
  10. What is the maximum total assignment (including the extra credit) score that you can achieve in this class?
  11. When and where are the instructor's office hours?

You should name your text file introduction.txt and upload it to the Introduction Assignment dropbox in Desire2Learn.  It is important that you use the dropbox, and avoid emailing the assignent to the instructor.

The second thing you should do in this assignment is upload a digital photograph of yourself to the dropbox.  Any photograph will do as long as it clearly shows your face and what you look like.  The photograph will be treated as confidential student information, and will not be distributed to the class.  The purpose of the photograph is to allow the instructor to recognize you if an in-person meeting is required, and for identification purposes if your class participation ever needs to be verified.

When you complete this assignment there should be two files in the Desire2Learn dropbox: the introduction.txt file and your digital photograph.
